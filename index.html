Trying to "Live"

Welcome to this mess of a website. To be frank, there is no point to this website. It's just a glimpse into the life of a random person on the Internet. People love that, right? Let's see if this works out.

About

Hi! I'm starting this blog for no reason. As of August 26th, 2018, I've begun programming this ugly mess. My hope is that this can be a platform for my thoughts. My interests include into activism, art, and science, so this may all culminate into an amalgamation of unrelated posts, but hopefully it'll all turn out well! Maybe I'll keep this private, or perhaps, if I work up the confidence, I'll make this a public blog... Only time will tell I suppose. Or else, maybe I'll completely abandon this whole project like I do with most things!
